# Responsible Use

FMG-Bench is a measurement tool for research and evaluation. It is not a
deployment license and does not certify that an AI system is safe or appropriate
for pastoral use.

Do not use FMG-Bench scores to claim that a model or product is:

- spiritually authoritative;
- pastorally endorsed;
- clinically safe;
- legally reliable;
- a replacement for clergy, counselors, physicians, lawyers, emergency services,
  family, or trusted community.

The benchmark measures response behavior under controlled scenarios. It does not
measure real user outcomes, real pastoral relationships, spiritual authority, or
clinical safety.

Any system used in faith and moral guidance contexts should remain bounded,
transparent, tradition-aware, safety-conscious, and subject to qualified human
oversight.
